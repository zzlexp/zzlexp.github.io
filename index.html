Zhong Li

Dr. Zhong Li is currently an Assistant Researcher at Software Institute, Nanjing University, and a member of Software Engineering Group, led by Prof. Xuandong Li.

He received his Ph.D. degree from Nanjing University in December 2023 under the supervision of Associate Professor Minxue Pan, Professor Tian Zhang, and Professor Linzhang Wang.

His current research interests include intelligent software engineering and trustworthy AI.
