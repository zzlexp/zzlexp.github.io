Zhong Li

Dr. Zhong Li is currently an Assistant Researcher (Yuxiu Young Scholar) at Software Institute, Nanjing University, and a member of Software Engineering Group, led by Prof. Xuandong Li.

He received his Ph.D. degree from Nanjing University in December 2023 under the supervision of Professor Minxue Pan, Professor Tian Zhang, and Professor Linzhang Wang.

His current research interests include intelligent software engineering and trustworthy AI.
